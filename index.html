Playbook

Outbound
Strategy

How I build an outbound motion from zero to pipeline — documented, tested, and repeatable.

Motion Type
Multi-channel outbound
Channels
LinkedIn · Email · Cold Call
Tools
Sales Navigator · Apollo · Lusha · Drippify · Outreach
Pilot Batch
100 accounts before automation
Methodology
Signal-led, ICP-filtered, A/B tested
01
Day 1–2 · Discovery
Product Deep-Dive & Documentation
First 24 hours go entirely to understanding the product. I document the MVP and USP — what problem it solves, who it's built for, and what makes it defensible. No outreach starts before I can articulate value in one sentence.
MVP mappingUSP documentationValue prop clarity
02
Week 1 · ICP Development
Build the ICP with Marketing
Collaborate with marketing to develop an Ideal Customer Profile — firmographics, technographics, buying triggers, and persona pain points. Cross-referenced against existing customer data to validate assumptions before locking it in.
FirmographicsPersona pain pointsCross-functional
03
Week 1 · Refinement
AI-Assisted ICP Filtering
Run the documented ICP through AI to tighten filtering criteria — removing noise, surfacing edge cases, and pressure-testing the profile against real-world account data. Output is a sharper, more precise ICP ready for tooling.
AI filteringCriteria refinementSignal validation
04
Week 1–2 · List Building
Sales Navigator — Saved Searches & Lead Lists
Apply the refined ICP directly into LinkedIn Sales Navigator using boolean filters, job title targeting, company size, tech stack, and growth signals. Build saved searches for a continuous feed and export an initial draft lead list for pilot testing.
Sales NavigatorBoolean searchSaved searchesLead list
05
Week 2 · Account Research
Signal Audit — First 100 Accounts
The first 100 accounts get individually researched — no spray-and-pray. Pull buying signals from Sales Navigator (job changes, growth, content activity) and cross-reference with 10-K filings via Companies House UK or SEC for enterprise targets. Context drives relevance.
Account signals10-K researchCompanies HouseIntent data
06
Week 2 · Messaging
Draft Copy & A/B Test Messaging
Develop A/B variants for each touchpoint — subject lines, opening lines, CTAs. Test against the pilot batch, track reply rates and positive sentiment, and iterate fast. No message gets scaled until proven at small batch level.
A/B testingCopywritingCTA testingReply rate tracking
07
Week 2–3 · Manual Execution
Ship the First 100–150 Manually
Before any automation, the first 100–150 touches go out manually. This forces quality control, surfaces friction in messaging, and builds genuine signal on what lands. Automation is earned — not assumed. Only after this phase is finalized does the sequence get templated.
Manual sendQuality controlMessage refinement
08
Week 3+ · Scale
Automate with Drippify · Extract Emails via Lusha
Once messaging is validated, use Drippify to automate sequences at scale and Lusha to extract verified contact emails for multi-channel reach. Automation runs while I focus on replies, live calls, and active pipeline management.
DrippifyLushaSequence automationEmail extraction
09
Ongoing · Multi-Touch Cadence
The Full Sequence
Every prospect goes through a structured multi-channel cadence. Each step is deliberate — no spam, no generic follow-ups. Timing, channel order, and messaging are continuously adjusted based on engagement data.
